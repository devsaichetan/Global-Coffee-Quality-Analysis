# 📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Quality Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze coffee quality metrics, production characteristics, sensory evaluation scores, and regional performance.

This dashboard transforms raw coffee quality data into actionable insights that help stakeholders understand quality trends, identify high-performing regions, evaluate processing methods, and support data-driven decision-making within the coffee industry.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Coffee quality is influenced by multiple factors including:

* Growing region
* Coffee variety
* Processing method
* Aroma
* Flavor
* Acidity
* Balance
* Sensory characteristics

Without proper analysis, identifying key quality drivers becomes difficult.

This project addresses this challenge by providing a centralized dashboard that enables users to explore coffee quality performance and discover meaningful business insights.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

* Premium coffee quality is strongly influenced by sensory attributes such as aroma, flavor, acidity, aftertaste, and balance.

* Certain coffee-producing regions consistently outperform others, highlighting the importance of geographic and environmental factors.

* Processing methods significantly impact final quality scores, making post-harvest practices a critical factor in coffee excellence.

* High-quality coffee batches exhibit greater consistency across multiple sensory dimensions.

* Specialty coffee markets can leverage these insights to optimize sourcing and quality assurance strategies.

* Data-driven quality evaluation helps producers, exporters, and roasters improve competitiveness and profitability.
